Frequently Asked Questions

Can I hire only digital services or only graphic services?

Yes. While we highly recommend combining both for the strongest result, you can engage strictly with Alpha Digital for development/systems, or Alpha Graphic for purely visual and motion deliverables.

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work primarily with established B2B and ambitious B2C companies looking to scale. This includes tech startups, healthcare providers, real estate firms, and retail brands needing robust digital operations and premium branding.

How long does a typical website project take?

A standard informational business website takes 4 to 6 weeks from discovery to launch. Complex web applications, automated dashboards, or heavy e-commerce builds typically range from 8 to 12 weeks.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

Absolutely. We don't believe in handing over a complex system and disappearing. We offer monthly retainer packages for priority hosting, security patches, system updates, and ongoing visual asset creation.

Can you improve an existing website instead of rebuilding it?

It depends on the current codebase. If your site is built on a modern, scalable stack, we can audit and improve it. If the underlying code is outdated, bloated, or structurally flawed, we will recommend a rebuild to ensure performance and security.

Privacy Policy

At Alpha Digital Solutions, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us while navigating our website or inquiring about our Digital and Graphic services.

1. Information We Collect

Because we offer custom agency services, the information we collect is strictly related to providing those services. We may collect:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and company details provided via our inquiry forms.
  • Project Details: Information regarding your budget, timeline, and business goals.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics such as browser type, pages visited, and interaction depth to help us improve our site experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively to:

  • Respond to your project inquiries and provide accurate quotes.
  • Deliver the requested Alpha Digital (software, hosting, web) or Alpha Graphic (design, video, 3D) services.
  • Improve our website's performance and usability.

3. Data Sharing and Protection

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share necessary data only with trusted third-party service providers (such as secure hosting providers or email routing services) strictly for the purpose of operating our business and delivering your project.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, or to ask us to delete your information from our active inquiry records. To do so, please contact us directly at hello@alphadigitalsolutions.com.

Terms & Conditions

Welcome to Alpha Digital Solutions. Because our services range from complex software engineering (Alpha Digital) to creative brand identity and motion (Alpha Graphic), specific project rules and deliverables will always be defined in your individual project proposal. However, these general terms apply to our engagements.

1. General Project Scope

The exact scope of work, deliverables, timeline, and cost will be outlined in a custom Statement of Work (SOW) or Proposal before any work begins. Any requests outside of the agreed scope will be subject to additional estimates.

2. Alpha Digital Services (Web & Systems)

  • Testing & Approval: Digital products are delivered with a testing period. Clients must report bugs within this timeframe.
  • Maintenance: Post-launch support, security updates, and hosting management require a separate, ongoing maintenance agreement. Without this, Alpha Digital is not liable for future server or software degradation.

3. Alpha Graphic Services (Design & Content)

  • Revisions: Graphic design and video projects include a set number of revision rounds as defined in the proposal. Further changes will be billed at an hourly rate.
  • Asset Ownership: Upon final and full payment, intellectual property rights for the finalized visual assets are transferred to the client. Alpha Digital Solutions retains the right to display the work in our portfolio.

4. Payment Terms

Custom projects require a non-refundable deposit to secure scheduling and commence work. The remaining balance is tied to project milestones or final delivery, as outlined in your specific agreement. Final assets or code deployment will not occur until the final invoice is cleared.
